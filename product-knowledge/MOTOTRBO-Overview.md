# MOTOTRBO Product Portfolio
## Digital Radio Solutions Overview

---

## Introduction

MOTOTRBO is Motorola Solutions' flagship digital two-way radio platform based on the Digital Mobile Radio (DMR) standard. It delivers integrated voice and data communications with enhanced features, increased capacity, and improved audio quality compared to analog systems.

---

## System Topologies

### 1. Single-Site Conventional
**Best For**: Small to medium deployments, campus operations

- **Coverage**: 20-50 km radius
- **Users**: Up to 100 per channel
- **Channels**: 1-16 repeaters
- **Cost**: Lowest infrastructure investment
- **Applications**: Factories, hotels, construction sites

### 2. IP Site Connect
**Best For**: Multi-location businesses, distributed sites

- **Coverage**: Regional (link via IP network)
- **Users**: Up to 240 per site
- **Sites**: Up to 15 interconnected
- **Cost**: Medium (uses existing IP infrastructure)
- **Applications**: Retail chains, logistics, municipalities

### 3. Capacity Plus Single-Site
**Best For**: High-density single location, campus trunking

- **Coverage**: 20-50 km radius
- **Users**: Up to 1,000 concurrent
- **Channels**: 1-16 frequency pairs
- **Cost**: Medium-high (trunking controller)
- **Applications**: Airports, ports, large manufacturing

### 4. Capacity Plus Multi-Site
**Best For**: Wide-area trunked coverage, large enterprises

- **Coverage**: Regional/national
- **Users**: Up to 3,000 system-wide
- **Sites**: Multiple interconnected
- **Cost**: High (distributed trunking)
- **Applications**: Oil & gas, mining, utilities, government

### 5. Linked Capacity Plus
**Best For**: Cost-effective wide-area trunking upgrade

- **Coverage**: Regional
- **Users**: Up to 1,500
- **Sites**: Multiple (simplified vs. CP Multi-Site)
- **Cost**: Medium (software upgrade from IP Site Connect)
- **Applications**: Growing businesses, phased trunking migration

### 6. Connect Plus
**Best For**: Large-scale metropolitan or national systems

- **Coverage**: Metropolitan/national
- **Users**: 3,000+ concurrent
- **Sites**: Unlimited
- **Cost**: Highest (full trunking infrastructure)
- **Applications**: Public safety, utilities, national fleets

#### SmartPTT
- **Function**: Advanced dispatch and fleet management
- **Users**: Oil & gas, energy, industrial
- **Features**: Voice recording, GPS AVL, SCADA integration, reporting
- **Market**: Mission-critical industries

### Broadband Push-to-Talk

#### WAVE PTX
- **Function**: Extend radio to smartphones, tablets, PCs
- **Technology**: Cloud-based PTT over LTE/5G/Wi-Fi
- **Integration**: Works with MOTOTRBO radios
- **Use Cases**: Supervisors, office staff, remote workers

### Safety Applications

#### Man Down
- **Function**: Automatic alert if worker falls or becomes immobile
- **Trigger**: Accelerometer + no movement timer
- **Alert**: Emergency priority call to dispatch

#### Lone Worker
- **Function**: Periodic check-in requirement
- **Trigger**: Timed interval (configurable)
- **Alert**: If no response, automatic emergency call

#### Emergency Alert
- **Function**: One-button emergency activation
- **Trigger**: Dedicated orange button
- **Alert**: Priority call with GPS location

### Data Applications

#### GPS Tracking (AVL)
- **Function**: Real-time location of all radios
- **Update**: Configurable (30 sec to 5 min intervals)
- **Display**: Dispatch console map view

#### Text Messaging
- **Function**: Silent communication via radio keypad
- **Types**: Free-form, canned messages, status updates
- **Use Cases**: Noisy environments, discreet coordination

#### Telemetry
- **Function**: Sensor data over radio network
- **Applications**: Tank levels, temperature, alarms, SCADA
- **Protocol**: IP data over DMR

## Migration Paths

### Analog to Digital
1. **Phase 1**: Deploy dual-mode (analog + DMR) radios
2. **Phase 2**: Add DMR repeaters alongside analog
3. **Phase 3**: Migrate users to digital operation
4. **Phase 4**: Decommission analog infrastructure

### Conventional to Trunked
1. **Start**: IP Site Connect (multi-site conventional)
2. **Upgrade**: Linked Capacity Plus (software upgrade)
3. **Expand**: Capacity Plus Multi-Site (as needs grow)
4. **Scale**: Connect Plus (if metro/national coverage needed)

✅ **2x Capacity**: Dual-slot DMR = two calls per frequency

## Certifications & Compliance

### Safety Certifications
- **ATEX**: Zone 1 and Zone 2 (explosive atmospheres - Europe)
- **IECEx**: International explosive atmospheres standard
- **UL**: Underwriters Laboratories (North America)
- **CSA**: Canadian Standards Association
- **INMETRO**: Brazilian certification

### Radio Standards
- **DMR**: ETSI TS 102 361 (Digital Mobile Radio)
- **FCC**: Part 90 (USA)
- **IC**: Industry Canada
- **CE**: European Union
- **MIC**: Japan

### Environmental
- **IP67/IP68**: Dust-tight and waterproof
- **MIL-STD-810**: Military environmental testing
- **IK**: Impact resistance rating

## Licensing & Spectrum

### Frequency Bands
- **VHF**: 136-174 MHz (better range, outdoor)
- **UHF**: 403-527 MHz (better building penetration)
- **900 MHz**: 896-941 MHz (specialized applications)

### License Types (varies by country)
- **Business/Industrial**: Standard commercial license
- **Public Safety**: Emergency services, government
- **Utilities**: Power, water, gas companies
- **Transportation**: Railways, aviation, marine

### Licensing Process (Indonesia)
Coordinated through **Balai Monitor Spektrum Frekuensi Radio** (BALMON) and **Kementerian Komunikasi dan Informatika** (Kominfo).
